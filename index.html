Booking & confirmation

A booking is only confirmed once we reply to confirm pickup — submitting the website form or sending a WhatsApp message starts a request, it does not by itself guarantee a rider has been assigned. Please provide accurate pickup and drop-off locations, a working phone number, and an honest description of the parcel's size, weight, and contents.

Inaccurate parcel information (wrong size, weight, or contents) may result in a revised quote, delayed pickup, or refusal of the item at the rider's discretion.

Payment is due on pickup or on delivery, as agreed at the time of booking (cash, mobile money, or another method we confirm with you). We reserve the right to hold a parcel until payment is settled.

Delivery timeframes & delays

We aim to complete same-day deliveries within Nairobi for bookings made within our operating hours. Timeframes are estimates, not guarantees. Delays can occur due to traffic, weather, road closures, incorrect address information, or the recipient being unavailable — we are not liable for delays caused by circumstances outside our reasonable control.

We take reasonable care of every parcel in transit. We are not liable for loss or damage arising from: inadequate packaging, inaccurate item description, prohibited items carried without our knowledge, or events beyond our reasonable control. Claims for loss or damage must be raised within 48 hours of the scheduled delivery time.
